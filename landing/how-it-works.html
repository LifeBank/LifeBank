How it works

LifeBank App connects the right blood donor to recipient anywhere they are in Nigeria.

This is how it works: a volunteer donor signs up with his/her location and blood group, and is immediately entered into the database of thousands of other Nigerians who are willing to donate blood whenever called.

When a patient needs blood, the volunteer that matches the blood type and whose location is closest to the clinic is alerted to save a life via email or a simple text message.

Our goal is to create a live-saving social network of volunteer donors and recipients around the country, thus eliminating the dangerous and prevalent presence of commercial middlemen that trade in what we have been given as a gift of life.
